Go Beyond The Impossible

Gurren Lagann is a fantastic anime and may perhaps be the best one of all time. The plot is simple, but brings new twists to the table and many mysteries to uphold. The characters are great and spectacular and all of their arcs are well done. Simon was a well written character who, because of Kamina's faith in him, defeated the biggest threat of the universe. And the same goes with all of the characters including Yoko, the best redhead sniper in anime, Nia, who goes through a beautiful dynamic with Simon, and the Black Siblings, Kittan, Kiyoh, Kinon and Kiyal, who are enjoyable as always. The same goes with everybody. But the best part are the mecha fights. The action sequences are phenomenal and are outrageously insane. It is gotten to the point where they fight other mechas on top of frikin galaxies, like this anime throws science books off the window. So long story short, it is an awesome anime that is one of my favorites of all time!
